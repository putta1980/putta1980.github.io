Our Story

About MRP Publications

MRP Publications is a technical publishing house specialising in computer science, data science, hyperspectral imaging, and software engineering. Founded by Dr. Puttaswamy M R, we are committed to producing high-quality educational resources that bridge theory and practice.

Our books are designed for students, researchers, and professionals who require structured, hands-on learning materials. Each title is crafted with clarity, depth, and practical application in mind.

"We believe that accessible, rigorous technical education has the power to transform careers and advance research."

All MRP Publications titles are available globally through Amazon in both Kindle and paperback formats, ensuring learners everywhere can access the knowledge they need.

We work closely with co-authors and subject-matter experts to ensure every book meets the highest standards of accuracy, pedagogy, and practical value.
